Introducing Launcher
Launcher empowers you to launch your own crowdfunding platform.
It is a starting point for an open-sourced Rails application. It was originally
created by NeverFold when Kickstarter rejected their project.
Why use Launcher?
  • It's open source and free!
  • Save hours and hours of coding.
  • No commission.
    (Kickstarter keeps 5% of raised funds)
